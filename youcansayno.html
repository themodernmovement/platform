// Process

How It Works

Simple. Transparent. Amplified.

01

Post Your Issue

Submit a data center proposal threatening your community. Include location, water impact, and what your commission is being asked to approve.

02

Community Votes

Anyone can vote Yes or No on any initiative. Votes are tallied publicly and sent as a report to the relevant local commission before their vote date.

03

Commission Notified

We send a certified community impact report to every commissioner on record, showing exactly how many constituents object — or support — the measure.

04

Track the Outcome

Win or lose, the vote outcome is recorded. Patterns across communities expose which corporations, lobbying groups, and commissioners are ignoring the public good.
